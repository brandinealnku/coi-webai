Interactive WebAI + AR

This experience demonstrates how modern webpages can respond intelligently in real time using camera input, face tracking, background replacement, and visual overlays. It is built to feel approachable for visitors while still showcasing meaningful AI and web technology.

How to use it

  1. 1 Tap Start Camera & AI and allow access to your device camera.
  2. 2 Try a mode such as Glasses, Helmet, Viking, or Griffin Hall.
  3. 3 Use Capture Snapshot to save a photo of your experience to your device.

What it demonstrates

• Camera access with getUserMedia
• Real-time face tracking with BlazeFace
• Background compositing with BodyPix
• Canvas-based overlays for interactive web experiences
• Mobile-friendly browser AI suitable for outreach and recruitment
