Student Custom Studio

Build Your Own WebAI Filter Experience

Upload your own images to turn this into a personalized interactive camera demo. Students can create a themed experience using their own background image and their own transparent PNG filter.

How students use it

  1. Upload a custom background image like a stadium, classroom, city, beach, or event scene.
  2. Upload a transparent PNG filter like glasses, a hat, a crown, a frame, or a creative overlay.
  3. Choose a mode: camera only, custom filter, custom background, or both together.
  4. Start the camera and test how their uploaded assets appear live.
  5. Capture a snapshot to save and share their result.
Best choice: a JPG or PNG photo. This will replace the real background behind the person.
