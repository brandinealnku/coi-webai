Technology you can actually experience

This page shows technology happening live in the browser. It helps students imagine what it feels like to create interactive, modern digital experiences.

Pathways for different kinds of students

You do not have to be “the coding kid” to belong here. Students with interests in art, communication, business, storytelling, gaming, AI, and problem-solving can all find a path.

Projects that feel real

Informatics is about doing meaningful work with technology—not just reading about it. Think building, testing, designing, presenting, creating, and solving.

Big takeaway for students

If you have ever thought, “I like technology, but I’m not exactly sure where I fit,” this is the kind of college space designed to help you discover that answer.
